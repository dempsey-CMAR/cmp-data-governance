Thresholds

Most QC test thresholds for dissolved oxygen were based on historical Coastal Monitoring Program data. Preliminary quality control was applied to the data1. Different thresholds were calculated for data measured in percent saturation and concentration. The number of observations collected and number of counties and stations monitored for each set of units is presented in Table 1. Station locations are shown in Figure 1.



For most counties, there are fewer “good” records in the spring and summer months, when conditions are conducive to biofouling2, and several counties have no data for at least one month (?@fig-do-n-obs-county-month).

Five counties have only one station with DO data (Figure 1).
